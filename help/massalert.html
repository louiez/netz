Mass Alert

A Mass alert is when NETz detects on one Monitor cycle that 
 many sites show down that were not down on the last cycle.

You can set this threshold Number that NETz will use to trigger an alert.

During Alert Check.. 
the number of sites down
that have not already Triggerd an alert (ALERT_SENT)
must be equal to or greater than this threshold number to trigger a mass alert.

Mass alert emails will continue until fixed or the monitor shut down.

During a mass alert... no other alerts will be sent ...nor will the ALERT_SENT flag be set.
